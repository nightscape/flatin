# Privacy Policy for Latin Practice

**Last updated:** November 19, 2024

## Introduction

Latin Practice ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application ("App").

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally
- **Practice Progress**: Your practice sessions, scores, and spaced repetition scheduling data are stored locally on your device
- **Settings**: Your app preferences and configuration settings
- **FSRS Cards**: Vocabulary cards and their review history are stored locally using SharedPreferences

### No Data Collection
- We do **not** collect, transmit, or store any personal information
- We do **not** use analytics or tracking services
- We do **not** collect device identifiers or location data
- We do **not** share data with third parties
- We do **not** use advertising or monetization services

## How We Use Information

All data is stored locally on your device and is used solely to:
- Track your learning progress
- Schedule vocabulary reviews using the FSRS algorithm
- Remember your app preferences
- Provide personalized practice sessions

## Data Storage

- All data is stored locally on your device
- Data is stored using Flutter's SharedPreferences
- You can clear all app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data through your device settings

## Data Security

- Since all data is stored locally, it never leaves your device
- We do not transmit any data over the internet
- Your data is protected by your device's security measures

## Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect any information from children. Since we do not collect any data, there is no risk of collecting information from children.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
- GitHub: https://github.com/nightscape/fluttin

## Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- You have full control over your data
- You can delete all data by uninstalling the app
- You can clear app data through your device settings at any time

---

**Note**: This app operates entirely offline and does not require an internet connection to function. All features work without network access.
